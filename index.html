ABOUT ME

Played High School Tennis at Albemarle High School, all district team. Played Club Tennis at Virginia Tech. Trained at Boars Head Sports Club in Charlottesville, Va, and Junior Tennis Champions Center in College Park, Md.

WHAT I'M HERE FOR

My name is Quentin Jenkins, and I am a tennis coach specializing in youth instruction. I also provide adult lessons, group lessons, racquet stringing, and am available as a hitting partner. I have been playing tennis since I was about 5 years old, played varsity at Albemarle High School and on the Virginia Tech Club Tennis team. I am a 5.0 level player and I have been coaching for about 4 years.

CERTIFICATIONS

Specialize in coaching high school players and young players who want to play competitively. Experienced working with children aged 5 and older of any skill level, as well as adults of any skill level.
